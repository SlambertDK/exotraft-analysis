The Honest Slide

Two adoption killers
that nobody has solved

🔩 The Mounting Problem
Drilling into a chimney pot requires roof access — roofer or specialist. Most homeowners won't attempt it. Trade visit costs €150–300 before the fan is even considered.
⚡ The Power Problem
Routing 230V to a chimney top is a full electrician job: 3–6 hours, permits, €400–800. Creates a second scheduling dependency — and a second veto point in the buying journey.
Concepts emerging: Solar-powered · TEG self-powered · Magnetic snap-mount · Extension pole

Installation Roadmap

From €1,200 installed
to €300 self-install

3 Months · No New Hardware
Extension Pole System
Pole-mount adapter allows ground-level or ladder installation. No roofer required. Every chimney sweep already owns telescoping rods — they become natural installers instantly.
12 Months · New Form Factor
Magnetic Snap-Mount + Battery Pack
Universal magnetic adapter for standard chimney pots — no drilling. Integrated battery removes the electrician entirely. DIY installation under 30 minutes. Opens the rental market.
24 Months · Zero Infrastructure
Solar ExoTraft — Full Self-Install
Integrated solar panel + TEG backup + battery. Ships fully assembled. Truly zero-infrastructure installation. Opens vacation homes, rentals, and the entire DIY segment across Europe.
